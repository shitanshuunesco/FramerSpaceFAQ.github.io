FramerSpace: Frequently Asked Questions

What is a FramerSpace?

FramerSpace is UNESCO MGIEP's indigenously designed, artificial intelligence (AI) powered digital co-creation platform. It helps curriculum designers, policy-makers, content developers, teachers and learners (13+) rethink how knowledge can be imparted in the 21st century. It provides building blocks to support the creation of online courses and connects learners to peers and creators through Artificial Intelligence.

Which device should I use to access FramerSpace: Smartphone or Laptop?

FramerSpace is optimized for laptops and desktops experience. One should ideally open FramerSpace on any browser on the laptops/ desktops.

How to find my course on FramerSpace?

The course coordinators from UNESCO MGIEP or your instructor should have shared the link to the course via email or message. Please use that link to reach to the correct course on FramerSpace.

Why am I not able to see the assessments on the course?

You are probably not on the right course. Please join your cohort on FramerSpace using this link shared with you through email.

What are the things that I should make sure of while I’m signing up?

  1. Unique ID (since this is needed for logging in)
  2. Your password (since this is needed for logging in)
  3. Adding email address (since this is needed for recovery purposes)

How do I add or update my email if I forgot to do it during the signup process?

You can go to the Reset Unique ID tab from your profile icon (top right corner) or you can use this link (to be added) to add or update your email. Find the guide in the interactive demo for more help: Click here!

How can I note my Unique ID if I forgot to do it during my sign-up?

You can go to the Reset Unique ID tab from your profile icon (top right corner) or you can use this link (to be added) to copy your ID. You can also use the Generate a new ID button if you want to generate a new Unique ID.

What happens if I lose my Unique ID?

You’ll lose your account and there would be no way to get it back.

What happens if I lose my password?

You can reset the password if you have added your email address for recovery. If you haven’t added your email, you won’t be able to recover your account.

How do I join my course?

Using the hyperlink provided by your facilitator or teacher. Note: your course is the customized course content designed for a particularly for your batch of learners. Your course is not one in the public home page, even if the name of the course on public home page is similar to your course.

Where can I find my course that I have already joined?

You can find your course in the “Draft Courses” tab under your profile icon (top right corner). The draft courses tab contains all the courses that you are part of. Note: you can also choose to bookmark the hyperlink of the cohort for ease of access in the future.

How do I customize my profile details?

You can click “Settings” tab under your profile icon (top right corner). After clicking, you will see options to type your profile details. You need to input your details in the appropriate text boxes and press “Update Profile Button”
